◆ calculateSampleErrorStats()

void Robot::calculateSampleErrorStats ( )

calculates the sample mean and sample variance of the error for all the odometry and tracking measurements.

The sample mean and sample variance are calculate as $$\begin{align} \bar{x} = \frac{\sum_{i\in n} x_i}{n}\ \sigma^2 = \frac{\sum_{i\in n} (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n-1} \end{align}$$ where \(x_i\) denotes the $i$-th element in the sample of size \(n\). The sample variance formulation uses Bessel correction.

Note
The calculation on the sample mean relies on the population of the Robot::error vector and therefore, Robot::calculateMeasurementError needs to be called before this function.
